Logo Hunt

About the event:

Design a digital logo for our Techfest- "Asymptotes" and send in your work by 23/10/2016 ,5:00 p.m at dtu.macs@gmail.com

Rules and Regulations:
  1. Your logo would be uploaded on our Facebook page on the evening of 23/10/16.
  2. Get maximum likes on MACS's Fb page as well as your logo.You will have ONE WEEK(23-30) to do so.
  3. Top two contestants with maximum likes on their entries will be awarded Rs.250 each.
  4. Only those likes on your entry would be counted who have liked MACS's FB page too. Likes achieved before 5 p.m, 30/10/2016 would be taken into consideration.
Prize:
  1. Apart from the above mentioned prizes,a panel will decide over one winner and he/she will be awarded Rs. 500 as well as a certificate.
  2. Moreover,if your logo really stands out then it may even get selected to represent our Techfest which will be held in Feb-2017.
